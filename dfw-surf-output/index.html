DFW SURF

Summer starts here! Since 2007 we have helped pioneer the two fastest growing watersports – stand up paddle boarding and wake surfing. Today we have three lakeside locations on Grapevine Lake and Lewisville Lake where you can hop on a boat, paddleboard or kayak, try wakesurfing, sup yoga, wing, wake and e-foiling, summer kids camps and more.
